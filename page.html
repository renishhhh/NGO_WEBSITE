Introduction to the NGO

Akshar Bharti is a thoughtful initiative that is committed to fostering the reading habits of underprivileged communities. This is achieved by providing access to high-quality non-academic literature. The initiative firmly believes in the transformative power of reading, which not only nurtures wisdom but also empowers individuals to make informed choices. Such empowerment leads to an enhanced quality of life and the development of responsible citizenship within these communities.

Vision

The vision is to align with India's aspiration to become a developed nation with sustainable growth. We aim to contribute to this vision by cultivating a culture of learning and education, empowering citizens of tomorrow to become agents of positive change.

Mission

The mission is to establish community libraries and active reading clubs in underprivileged areas across India. By doing so, we seek to instill a love for reading and self-development, thereby empowering individuals and communities to unlock their full potential and contribute meaningfully to the country's progress.

Akshar Bharati is an initiative to nurture the reading habits in children between the age group 5-15 years from underprivileged and remote areas by facilitating access to good non-academic literature. We set up libraries for such children in their localities so that the books are available when they need them the most. We strongly believe that quality reading develops the wisdom to make appropriate choices as one grows which ultimately enhances the quality of life by becoming better citizens. This is our way of contributing to India's Vision 2020 of being a developed and self-sufficient nation.

Our Initiatives

Junior Aryabhatta

Our Junior Aryabhatta was a Computer training program that catered to teaching the basic computer skills to young school students. We started this initiative in January 2017. Around 20 schools and more than 6000 students showed participation in the initiative.

  • Paint
  • MS Word
  • MS Excel
  • MS PowerPoint

Junior Kalam Program

After seeing the huge success of the Junior Aryabhatta Program, our team decided to launch the Junior Kalam Program on 15th August 2021. This program is specially curated for the underprivileged school students who lack access to certain opportunities important for skills development.

  • Computer education
  • Book reading
  • Scientific toys
  • Volunteering
  • Covid awareness

Bal Gokulam

Our Balgokulam program is the biggest event of the year. The main aim is to nurture the reading habit in students. Our program also involves:

  • Cultural programs
  • Scientific toys activity
  • Storytelling
  • Fun games

Jr. Kalam Club
